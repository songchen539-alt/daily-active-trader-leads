# Broker Lead Intelligence - Fast-Money MVP Offer

## Positioning

We provide qualified trader, investor, affiliate and IB leads for FX, CFD, crypto, stock and precious metals platforms.

This is not sold as raw scraped personal data. It is sold as a broker growth service with source logging, lead type classification, intent scoring, compliance labels and CRM-ready delivery.

## Who To Sell First

Sell to these roles first:

- Head of Affiliates at FX/CFD brokers
- Country Manager at brokers expanding in LATAM, SEA, MENA or Africa
- Growth Manager at crypto exchanges
- CMO at online brokerages
- Affiliate Manager at prop trading firms
- Founder of trading education platforms

## First Product To Sell

### Daily Active Lead Feed

Price: USD 2,000-8,000 per month

Includes:

- daily updated active leads
- 30-200 fresh records per day, depending on region and product
- hot_24h, warm_72h and cooling_7d freshness labels
- last seen timestamp
- activity signal, such as form submit, guide download, webinar registration, recent creator post, community activity or broker comparison page visit
- Targeting by product: FX, CFD, crypto futures, stocks, gold or IB/affiliate
- Targeting by region: LATAM, SEA, MENA, Africa
- CSV, Airtable, HubSpot or Google Sheets delivery
- Email verification status
- Source type and intent signal
- Compliance status label
- Recommended next action

Best for: platforms that need sales teams to call, message or route leads every day.

Daily feed is more valuable than a static database because brokers care about timing. A lead who downloaded a crypto futures guide today or joined a gold webinar yesterday is worth more than a generic investor contact from six months ago.

## Daily Active Feed Logic

Every record should have freshness and activity fields:

- feed_date
- last_seen_at
- freshness_tier: hot_24h, warm_72h, cooling_7d, stale
- activity_signal
- activity_score from 0 to 100
- product_interest
- country and language
- contact validation status
- compliance status
- recommended offer
- next action

Examples of activity signals:

- downloaded a trading checklist
- used a margin, pip, liquidation or position-size calculator
- registered for a trading webinar
- clicked a broker comparison page
- posted trading education content in the last 7 days
- runs an active Telegram, Discord, YouTube, TikTok, X or TradingView channel
- searched or engaged around MT4, MT5, crypto futures, gold CFD, stock broker or prop trading topics
- company recently hired affiliate, growth or country manager roles

## Lead Status Labels

Use these labels in every delivery:

- consented_lead: filled a form, downloaded an asset or registered for a webinar
- prospect_not_opted_in: enriched B2B or public professional prospect requiring compliant outreach
- legitimate_interest_review: B2B decision-maker that should be reviewed before outreach
- restricted_region: do not market unless client confirms permission
- suppression_match: do not contact

## Discovery Questions

Ask every potential client:

- Which products are you acquiring users for: FX, CFDs, crypto, stocks, gold, options or multi-asset?
- Which countries are allowed by your compliance team?
- Do you want trader leads, signups, KYC users, first deposits, affiliates or IBs?
- Can you accept enriched B2B prospects, or only consented inbound leads?
- What CRM do you use?
- What payout model do you prefer: CPL, CPA, FTD, RevShare or retainer?
- What is your minimum acceptable lead quality definition?

## Crypto Payment Setup

The public site now supports a crypto invoice request flow.

Recommended first payment methods:

- USDT TRC20 for low-fee international payments
- USDC ERC20 or Polygon for stablecoin settlement
- BTC for clients that prefer Bitcoin treasury payment
- ETH for EVM-native clients

Before collecting live payments:

- replace wallet placeholders in `index.html`
- confirm the exact network for each asset
- add a manual invoice number to every payment request
- require clients to submit TxID / transaction hash after payment
- reconcile payment before delivering lead files
- consider Coinbase Commerce, NOWPayments or CoinPayments if you want hosted checkout, exchange-rate locking and invoice links

Never publish a wallet address unless you have verified it belongs to you and supports the displayed network.
